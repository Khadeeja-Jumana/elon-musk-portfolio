Elon Musk


Elon Musk is a prominent entrepreneur, engineer, and inventor known for founding and leading multiple high-profile technology companies.Born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa.Musk developed an interest in computers and technology from an early age. He taught himself computer programming and sold his first software, a game called Blastar, at the age of 12.He attended Queen's University in Canada for two years before transferring to the University of Pennsylvania in the U.S. There, he earned degrees in both economics and physics.


Achievements

  • Became one of the world’s wealthiest individuals, often holding the title of the richest person based on net worth.
  • Joined Tesla in 2004 as investor and chairman, became CEO in 2008, leading the company to success in electric vehicles and renewable energy.
  • Founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.
  • Achieved the world’s first successful landing of a reusable orbital-class rocket with SpaceX’s Falcon 9 in 2015.
  • Was named to the Time 100 most influential people in the world multiple times.
